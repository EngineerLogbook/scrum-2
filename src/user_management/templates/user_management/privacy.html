Privacy Policy



Generic Privacy Policy

This privacy policy will help you understand how Engineering Logbook uses and protects the data you provide to us when you visit and use Engineering Logbook.
We reserve the right to change this policy at any given time, of which you will be promptly updated. If you want to make sure that you are up to date with the latest changes, we advise you to frequently visit this page.

What User Data We Collect

When you visit the Engineering Logbook, we may collect the following data:
a. Your IP address.
b. Your email address.
c. Other information such as interests and preferences.
d. Data profile regarding your online behavior on our Engineering Logbook.

Why We Collect Your Data?

We are collecting your data for several reasons:
a. To better understand your needs.
b. To improve our services.
c. To send you promotional emails containing the information we think you will find interesting.
d. To contact you to fill out surveys and participate in other types of market research.
e. To customize our Engineering Logbook according to your online behavior and personal preferences.
f. Safeguarding and Securing the Data

Engineering Logbook is committed to securing your data and keeping it confidential. Engineering Logbook has done all in its power to prevent data theft, unauthorized access, and disclosure by implementing the latest technologies and software, which help us safeguard all the information we collect online.

Cookie Policy

Once you agree to allow our Engineering Logbook to use cookies, you also agree to use the data it collects regarding your online behavior (analyze web traffic, web pages you spend the most time on, and Engineering Logbooks you visit).
The data we collect by using cookies is used to customize our Engineering Logbook to your needs. After we use the data for statistical analysis, the data is completely removed from our systems.
Please note that cookies don't allow us to gain control of your computer in any way. They are strictly used to monitor which pages you find useful and which you do not so that we can provide a better experience for you.

Links to Other websites:

Our Engineering Logbook contains links that lead to other website. If you click on these links, Engineering Logbook is not held responsible for your data and privacy protection. Visiting those website is not governed by this privacy policy agreement. Make sure to read the privacy policy documentation of the websites you go to from our Engineering Logbook.

Restricting the Collection of your Personal Data

At some point, you might wish to restrict the use and collection of your personal data. You can achieve this by doing the following:
When you are filling the forms on the Engineering Logbook, make sure to check if there is a box which you can leave unchecked, if you don't want to disclose your personal information. If you have already agreed to share your information with us, feel free to contact us via email and we will be more than happy to change this for you. Engineering Logbook will not lease, sell or distribute your personal information to any third parties, unless we have your permission. We might do so if the law forces us. Your personal information will be used when we need to send you promotional materials if you agree to this privacy policy.
